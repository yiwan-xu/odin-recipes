Gelato

Description

With distinct flavours of gelato present in different regions of Italy, this dessert is a must-try for any visitor to Italy, as you indulge in the freshness and creaminess brought by the gelato experience.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups x Whole milk
  • 1 cup x Granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp x Cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon x Vanilla paste
  • 1/4 tsp x Salt

Other brilliant additions:

  • Melted chocolate
  • Crushed berries
  • Pistachio cookies
  • Ginger cookies
  • Hot milk infused with mint/jasmine tea/saffron etc.

... and other variations to your liking!

Steps

  1. Place 3 cups of milk and the vanilla paste into a saucepan and brim to a simmer.
  2. As the mixture simmers, place a cup of milk into a large cup and whisk in the sugar, then whisk in the cornstarch.
  3. When the milk simmers, remove from heat and pour the cornstarch mixture into the milk. Place back on medium heat and stir, stir, stir, until things come back up to a simmer and thicken for 10 minutes or so.
  4. Strain (put through a sieve) the mixture into a container, letting it cool for 20 minutes, then put into the refrigerator for a few hours until chilled.
  5. Take out the gelato immediately to serve, or keep in freezer for a couple more hours.
