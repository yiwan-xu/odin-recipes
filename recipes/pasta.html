Pasta

Description

Pasta, as a delicious dish well-known in Italy and popularised all around the world, this dish holds its great aromas and tastes in the most down-to-earth ingredients, where every household is likely to have heard of its name, and, perhaps tried and essence of the goodness that remains within.

Ingredients

  • Plain flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

...or just shop-bought ready-made pasta if you'd like...

  • Water
  • Pasta sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley
  • (optional) (more) Salt & Pepper
  • (optional) Vegetables of your pick

Steps(with shop-bought pasta)

  1. Place a pot on the stove.
  2. Pour a sufficient amount of water into the pot, just over half the capacity of the pot.
  3. Switch the stove on to boil the water, and then wait for the water to boil.
  4. Once the water reaches boiling point, add 3 handfuls (or more depending on your desired servings) of pasta into the pot.
  5. Wait around 5-10 minutes to check if the pasta is ready, and switch the stove off once it is.
  6. Carefully, using the lid of the pot or otherwise, tip out the water from cooking the pasta.
  7. Pour 4 tablespoons of pasta sauce into the bowl and mix well. Add cut-up vegetables like onions or pepper and salt into the mixture if desired.
  8. Sprinkle some parmesan cheese onto the warm pasta, and you are done!
