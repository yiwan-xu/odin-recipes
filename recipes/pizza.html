Pizza

Description

A delicious quick-bite for a family meal or sharing with friends, pizzas are the go-to plan for a meal downtown. With a rich variety of toppings available, this makes the dining experience one-of-a-kind.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce (note: not ketchup!!)
  • Cheese
  • Basil

(additional)

  • Pepperoni
  • Meat (chicken, ham, beef, etc.)
  • Vegetables (onion, sweetcorn, tomato, etc.)
  • Pineapple (controversial)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 240 degrees celcius.
  2. Grab a palm-sized piece of dough and place it on the tray for baking. Use your hands to spread the dough out by pressing and stretching it into a circle shape.
  3. Using a spoon, spread some pizza sauce onto the dough so that it covers all of the dough, leaving the edges of the dough clean for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle cheese (or no cheese if lactose intolerant) and other toppings over the dough so that there is an adequate amount of toppings on the pizza.
  5. Put oven gloves on and place the tray into the oven for 10 minutes until the crust is golden-brown.
  6. Take the tray out with oven gloves, and use a pizza cutter to cut the pizza into equal pieces. Your pizza is now ready!
